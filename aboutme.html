Who Am I?

My name is Hejer, a university student in my twenties studying history. I have a deep love for history and a passion for sharing it through my content creation. Every post, every podcast, is a journey into the past, a story waiting to be told.

As I navigate through my university life, I continue to share these historical narratives online, hoping to inspire and educate others. This is more than just a hobby - it’s a passion that intertwines with my academic pursuits, enriching my understanding of the world. And as I continue to grow and learn, I look forward to sharing more of these stories with you.
